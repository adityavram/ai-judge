# APDA Speaker Scale (2021)

## Overview
- Speaker scores range from ~5 (awful) to ~45 (amazing), most cluster around 25 (average)
- Whole numbers only
- Scores below 15 or above 35 require justification to tab staff
- Ranks: 1 (best) to 4 (worst)
- No "low-point wins" — losing team's cumulative speaks must be equal or lesser than winning team
- Choose the LOWEST category a speech falls into (limited by weakest criterion)

## Scale Criteria

| Score Range | Warrant Quality | Impact Quality | Weighing Quality | Engagement | Argument Quality |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| **5-7** | Non-existent or no reason to believe claim | Non-existent, vague/unweighable, or predicated on unwarranted claims | Not done or merely asserted | Crucial round-winning material unresponded to, substantially impaired ability to win | Speech did MORE to help other side win than silence would have |
| **8-12** | Same as 5-7 | Same as 5-7 | Same as 5-7 | Same as 5-7 | Speech was net equivalent to silence (helped both sides equally) |
| **13-17** | Little to no reason to believe claim | Connected to warranting but vague or difficult to understand | Not done or merely asserted | Some potentially round-winning material unresponded to, substantially impaired ability to win | Weak enough to be beaten by an average person |
| **18-22** | Limited reason to have confidence in claims | Connected to warranting but importance somewhat vague | Partially done with insufficient justification | Some important material unresponded to, substantially impaired ability to win | Unable to withstand quality argumentation from other team |
| **23-27** | Substantial but not decisive reason to have confidence | Connected to warranting, precise degree/kind sometimes unclear | Done completely but with insufficient justification | No important material unresponded to, but more engagement would certainly increase ability to win | Strong despite exploitable flaws |
| **28-32** | Nearly decisive reason to have confidence | Connected to warranting with explanation of degree/kind where most crucial | Weighing within each impact type done completely but with insufficient justification | No important material unresponded to, but more engagement likely to have increased ability to win | Strong with minimal exploitable flaws |
| **33-37** | Decisive reason to have confidence in claims | Connected to warranting with consistent explanation of degree and kind | Weighing within each impact type done completely with reasonable (though limited) justification | No important material unresponded to, nor would more engagement have substantially increased ability to win | Strong with no clearly exploitable flaws |
| **38-40** | Decisive reason, and decisive in context of opposing warrants | Clear impacts including degree/kind, tailored to match weighing to establish RFD | Weighing within each impact type done completely, fully developed with justification | No important material unresponded to, nor would more engagement have substantially increased ability to win | Strong with no exploitable flaws |
| **41+** | (All 38-40 criteria) PLUS: argumentation/warranting/impacting/weighing so effective that judge has near-certain confidence this speech could win the round even against two flawless debaters, making up for partner's flaws | — | — | — | — |
